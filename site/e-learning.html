Study Notes

With a robust and up-to-date e-library, the students can access notes and other learning material from anywhere, easily.

Assignments

Students can receive and submit assignments online. The assignments are marked and results posted on the students and parents pages.

Students can do quizzes and exams online

SIMPLE | SECURE | RELIABLE

With the online system, students can do timed exams from anywhere. This helps us keep track of and improve the learning experience for our students.

Results are avaialable quickly and in some cases, even instantly. This provides opportunity for more targeted one-to-one discussion between teachers and students.

Keep track of student performance easily

Tracking performance is essential to improving results. Using the e-learning system, it's easier to keep track of how students are performing in classwork, homework, quizzes and exams.

Information is shared by the teacher to the parents and students. This helps parents stay in the loop on the student's performance and allows for greater collaboration both at home and at school.
