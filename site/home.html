The School of Choice

What makes us exceptional?

We ensure that our students develop both the skills that a sound education provides and the competencies essential for success and leadership in the emerging creative economy.

We lead in generating practical and theoretical knowledge that enables people to better understand our world and improve conditions for local and global communities

Explore PAN NATION ACADEMY

EDUCATION. INNOVATION. GROWTH

We are and will be a school where design and social research drive approaches to studying issues of our time, such as democracy, urbanization, technological change, economic empowerment, sustainability, migration, and globalization.

Our vision aligns with shifts in the global economy, society, and environment, which animates our mission and our values:

EDUCATION

We will be the preeminent intellectual and creative center for effective engagement in a world that increasingly demands better-designed objects, communication, systems, and organizations to meet social needs.

GROWTH

Social engagement should orient students' academic experiences to help them become critically engaged citizens, dedicated to solving problems and contributing to the public good.

INNOVATION

Creativity, innovation, and a desire to challenge the status quo will affect what and how we teach and the intellectual ambitions of the school itself.

OUR E-LEARNING PROGRAM

We provide a robust e-learning program to faciliate learning with ease and to ensure all stakeholders are in sync.

Leveraging technology for success

We've integrated a robust software system to complement our education efforts. With tools for students, parents and teachers, we're making it easier to communicate, collaborate and succeed.

Great for Students

From providing an easy to use e-library to submitting assignments online and even doing quizzes, remote learning can be even more impactful.

Ideal for Parents

Keep track of student performance over time, talk to teachers easily and stay in tune with school events and information with ease.
